Bayesian and frequentist statistics

Examples of projects where I used interesting statistical approaches

Non-linear models

A set of Bayesian non-linear models to study skewed and fat-tailed distributions as a way to understand presence/absence and abundance data while accounting for prior or indigenous knowledge.
Repository

Longitudinal data

Using several survival and generalized linear models, I studied the immune response of patients to SARS-CoV-2 infections and vaccination, untangling the differences across vaccine types, patients, and virus variants.

Plant-pollinator dynamics

I used Bayesian multinomial logistic regressions as well as network analysis to characterize the dynamics of empirical plant-pollinator interactions across seasons.
